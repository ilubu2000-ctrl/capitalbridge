Do I Qualify for
an SBA Loan?

SBA loans are available to most U.S.-based small businesses. Here are the general requirements:

  • Operate as a for-profit business in the United States
  • Meet SBA's definition of a "small" business
  • Reasonable owner equity (some investment in the business)
  • Credit score of 620+ (640+ preferred)
  • No recent bankruptcies or delinquencies on federal debt
  • Business operating for at least 2 years (exceptions for startups)
  • Demonstrate ability to repay the loan
