Do I Qualify?

General SBA eligibility requirements:

  • For-profit U.S. business
  • Meets SBA "small business" definition
  • Credit score 620+ (640+ preferred)
  • 2+ years in business (startup exceptions)
  • No recent bankruptcies or federal delinquencies
  • Owner is U.S. citizen or permanent resident
  • Demonstrated ability to repay
